AgReason: A Benchmark for Agricultural Reasoning

Iowa State University — Conference Name & Year

Abstract

We introduce AgReason, a domain-specific benchmark that probes how well large language models (LLMs) perform on agronomy—an area where general-purpose QA tests provide little insight.

  • AgThoughts Dataset — 44.4K expert-seeded questions with step-by-step LLM-generated answers screened for correctness.
  • AgReason Benchmark — a 100-item gold set covering 10 agronomic categories, validated by experts and using an LLM-as-judge.
  • AgThinker-8B — fine-tuned Phi3 8B model that narrows the gap to expert agronomists by 10 percentage points.
